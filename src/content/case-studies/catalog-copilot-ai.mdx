<h2>Overview</h2><p>Microsoft sellers have access to a catalog of solutions to sell to customers. There are thousands of solutions consisting of a mix of previously successful pitch decks, technical materials, product demos, proposals, case studies, and more. </p><p>Navigating this vast array of resources is<strong> time-consuming</strong>, creating inefficiencies in preparing materials for stakeholder presentations.</p><p>‚Äç</p><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" /><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><h2>Sellers face several challenges:</h2><ul><li><strong>Scattered tools:</strong> Sellers use multiple platforms, making it hard to collect the right information quickly.</li><li><strong>Manual processes:</strong> Preparing materials for customers requires manually pulling together resources, which wastes time and reduces productivity.</li><li><strong>Inconsistent workflows:</strong> The lack of unified processes across tools leads to slow customer decisions and missed sales opportunities.</li><li><strong>Adoption issues:</strong> Sellers struggle to adopt new tools that don't integrate seamlessly into their existing workflows, which affects consistency in sales efforts.</li></ul><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" src="https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65b85fef8d2d05d93341196f/66abec4749c4cae9b2d998b2_66abec38438fc27d1d4fddb6_msft-ecosystem-landscape%25201.png" width="auto" height="auto" loading="lazy" /><p>‚Äç</p><h2>Goal</h2><p>Our goal was to put Microsoft sellers back in control of their engagements by empowering them to:</p><ul><li>Quickly find <strong>relevant solutions</strong> across platforms</li><li><strong>Orchestrate teams effectively</strong> with unified workflows</li><li><strong>Make faster, informed decisions</strong> using data-driven insights from the AI copilot</li></ul><p>These goals were aligned with our company's OKRs, focusing on increasing seller productivity, improving cross-team collaboration, and driving customer satisfaction. </p><p>‚Äç</p><p>By positioning sellers as orchestrators of seamless customer experiences, we aimed to not only drive efficiency but also deliver customer value with ethical AI considerations in mind.</p><p>‚Äç</p><p>For the MVP, we narrowed our focus to one key persona: the specialist seller, despite having multiple seller personas.</p><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><h2>"As a seller, time is money. We need tools that not only save us time but offer consistency across platforms, so we can focus on closing deals faster."</h2><p>‚Äç</p><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" src="https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65b85fef8d2d05d93341196f/66a952b74127db7b1da0cac1_65f46375efd57b54aa5cb8a8_2-1.jpeg" width="auto" height="auto" loading="lazy" /><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><h2><strong>Solution:</strong></h2><p>We developed an <strong>AI Copilot Plugin</strong>, designed as a <strong>seamless assistant</strong> integrated into the seller‚Äôs daily applications. The plugin provides:</p><ul><li><strong>Actionable insights</strong> and <strong>next-best actions</strong> based on customer engagement</li><li><strong>Natural language processing</strong> to help sellers generate content and solutions faster</li><li><strong>Cross-platform functionality</strong> to ensure sellers work fluidly between tools</li></ul><p>This solution enables sellers to meet their customers where they are, while also maximizing sales productivity.</p><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" src="https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65b85fef8d2d05d93341196f/66a952b74127db7b1da0ca7e_65f31eb5f412f9676f737b4b_cropped-OMB_Hero-WholeNewWayToWork.jpeg" width="auto" height="auto" loading="lazy" /><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><p>‚Äç</p><h3>Design Approach</h3><p>To build this solution, we leveraged <strong>Microsoft‚Äôs Fluent Design System</strong> and existing copilot patterns to ensure consistency across the Microsoft ecosystem.
