<h2>What was delivered?&nbsp;</h2><p>✔️ Ideation +&nbsp;Brainstorming </p><p>✔️ Persona creation</p><p>✔️ Branding exploration</p><p>✔️ Information Architecture</p><p>✔️ Card UI&nbsp;design </p><p>✔️ Landing and Search UX&nbsp;design </p><p>‍</p><p>‍</p><h2>Now, let's start with the problem.</h2><p>There is a lack of a central place as a “source of truth” where sellers can efficiently find re-useable IP assets (delivery oriented or pre-sales oriented) resources that are complete and of high quality. </p><p>In addition to this:</p><ul><li>Sellers are currently insufficiently deconstructing and sifting through previously sold deals.</li><li>Deficiency in personalization</li><li>The absence of an existing scalable design system. </li></ul><p>‍</p><p>‍</p><h2>Impact as a result of the problem</h2><p>Microsoft Sellers often experience a fragmented and disconnected sales ecosystem, leading to inefficient, confusing, and disjointed customer engagements.</p><p>‍</p><p>‍</p><h2>How can we fix this?</h2><p>A central repository providing enterprise sellers with a comprehensive view of Microsoft’s professional services offerings to reusable customer-centric sales teams. </p><p>‍</p><p>The redesigned web experience will empower Sellers to achieve business targets faster through a simplified, intelligent, trusted experience.</p><p>‍</p><ul><li>Establishing a <strong>scalable</strong> design system.</li><li>Accelerated time to close a deal. Assist users to quickly and easily <strong>find relevant offers </strong>based on a customer needs. </li><li>Proactively surface more <strong>relevant, personalized content</strong> to sellers.</li><li>Facilitates the easy identification of <strong>trending assets</strong> within the seller catalog.</li></ul><p>‍</p><p>‍</p><h2>Humanizing the problem</h2><p>This involved creating detailed and relatable representations of our target audience, the <strong>Microsoft Seller</strong>.
